About

Pragmasol Consultancy

Pragmasol is a Software Development Consultancy based in the UK.

Our core areas of expertise are:

  • Data Engineering
  • Distributed Systems
  • Scalability
  • Cloud Infrastructure
  • Mentoring and Coaching

We believe that every problem should be approached with an open mind and that solutions should be delivered using the correct tools for the job. We believe in quality over quantity and concise elegant code that is readable, well documented and easy to maintain. In addition to this we believe that everything starts at the requirement level and if requirements are not well understood or defined then any software built based on such requirements will have similar characteristics ie not fit for purpose. We don’t believe in body-shopping just to get software and infrastructure delivered as quickly as possible because inevitably quality suffers and technical debt is incurred as a result which, in our experience, is ten times harder to pay back later on. Opportunity costs are obviously important to consider and pragmatism is required though quality should not be forsaken just to meet a deadline.

If our approach aligns with your goals and expecations then please get in touch to arrange a consultation to see how we can help you.
